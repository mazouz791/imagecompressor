Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 12, 2026

Image Compressor ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit your personal information to our servers.

Photos and Media Access

The App requests access to your photos and media only to:

  • Select images that you choose to compress.
  • Save compressed images back to your device.

All image processing is performed locally on your device. Your images are never uploaded to our servers.

Camera Permission

If you choose to capture a new image using your camera, the App requests camera permission only for that purpose. Images remain on your device.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • IP address
  • App usage information

For more information about how Google processes data, please visit:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated revision date.
